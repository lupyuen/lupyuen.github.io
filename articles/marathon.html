                            How to get started with Marathon Training?

                            Most 6-month Marathon Training Plans will look a bit like this. For Every Week: We do‚Ä¶

                            • One (Slower) Long Run (up to 32 km)

                            • One (Faster) Short Run (like 5 km)

                            • Eventually hitting 42 km Total Mileage per week (almost)

                            2. Legs are seriously aching!

                              Remember to breathe deep, stretch the diaphragm. Drinking too much water might cause leg cramps. But if we‚Äôre peeing too little during a run, please drink up right now!

                            3. Abrasion of thighs, nipples, butt, shoulder, ‚Ä¶

                            ¬ß11 Appendix: Taper Before Marathon

                            What‚Äôs this tapering?

                            3 Weeks before the Actual Marathon, we taper down or reduce the mileage and intensity of our Marathon Training (to prevent over-training). Why 3 weeks?

                            • Risk Management: Most Running Injuries will (probably) take at most 3 weeks to heal. Makes sense to taper down our running 3 weeks before a Marathon, in case we injure ourselves during training.

                            • 30-Day Window: Research says that we shouldn‚Äôt exceed 10% of the distance of our last Long Run over the past 30 days. So we should probably run 42 km, then taper down 3 weeks before the Marathon.
