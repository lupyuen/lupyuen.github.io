                                ¬ß5 Marathon Training Plan

                                How to get started with Marathon Training?

                                Most 6-month Marathon Training Plans will look a bit like this. For Every Week: We do‚Ä¶

                                • One Long (Slower) Run (up to 32 km)

                                • One Short (Faster) Run (like 5 km)

                                • Eventually hitting 42 km Total Mileage per week (almost)

                                • 3 Weeks Before Actual Marathon: Taper Down the Total Mileage

                                  (More about Tapering for Marathon)

                                BUT to prevent injury from overtraining‚Ä¶

                                1. Every Week: We increase at most 10% of the Weekly Mileage OR Intensity

                                2. Don‚Äôt exceed 10% of the distance of our last Long Run over the past 30 days (TODO link)

                                3. Remember to do Strength Training. Gym not necessary, dumbbells will do. (Think: Squats with Dumbbells, great for strengthening the groin)

                                What‚Äôs the distance for our First Long Run? 10% Weekly Increment: How soon will it reach 42 km?

                                Ah remember that 10% is the Compounded Weekly Increment‚Ä¶

                                • Suppose we‚Äôre comfortable with hiking 20 km

                                • We extend the distance every week by 10%
